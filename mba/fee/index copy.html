KSBL charges fee per credit hour, applicable as per the total number of credit hours in a Program
Note
  • If a student's annual dues surpass PKR 200,00/-, an advance tax of 5% on the whole amount (under section 236 I) will be collected as per the revision in Income Tax Ordinance 2001 by Finance Act 2013
  • Advance taxes are adjustable at the time of filing an income tax return. Advance tax certificates will be issued by KSBL at the end of the year
  • The fee structure does not include housing, food, government taxes or any other additional facilities
  • Fee structure does not vary for international students
