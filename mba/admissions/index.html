Admissions Process

Admission at KSBL is based on various parameters of competencies and diversities of applicants. Here you will understand how the admissions process works, from completing your application, to reaching the interview stage. It is a simple but holistic three step process:

1. Filling out all sections of our online application form
2. Assessment (Including KMAT)
3. Interview

About KMAT
KMAT Registration

The KSBL Management Admission Test (KMAT) is designed to assess the quantitative, verbal, analytical, and writing skills of applicants to KSBL graduate programs.

Scoring: Test is scored on absolute and percentile scales. For evaluations in graduate admissions, percentile scores are considered.

Validity: Test scores are valid for two (2) years from date of test.

Candidates can opt for other tests in lieu of KMAT including GMAT, GRE and NTS-GAT.
