Chartered by Government of Sindh

The Sindh Provincial Assembly passed KSBL institute bill in December 2012 with a unanimous vote, authorizing the institution to award degrees in business-related disciplines.

The Mission

The quest undertaken by KSBL is to bring a value-added and holistic business education degree program. Our academic system is structured to facilitate young talent with a globally-recognized curriculum to reform them into world leaders who can amend conventional ways of leading.

The Vision

In order to continue with our reputation for quality education, the vision of KSBL comprises of these core elements:
Facilitating learners with an ultra-modern campus-site and learning facilities
A renowned faculty with a strong academic background and equipped with modern-day skills to deliver education at par with international standards
Promoting diversity and inclusion across gender, geography, socio-economic strata, race and ethnic backgrounds
Acquiring international accreditation and incorporating international educational standardization in order to be noted amongst the leading business schools
Equipping talented professionals with valuable assets through modern-day learning that brings together creativity, leadership potential and a problem-solving attitude
