JOBS AT KSBL

KSBL is looking to hire a Teaching Assistant for the department of Decision Sciences.

Establish and maintain robust financial and accounting systems, policies and procedures, develop financial planning and budgeting processes, design and monitor reliable control systems, ensuring compliance with statutory requirements and accounting standards...

Job Description:

Perform teaching assistant (TA) duties supporting the Head of the Department with his classes and labs, such as helping students with problems in debugging code and aiding instructor in-lab assessments such as exams and programming, and administering in-class quizzes. Able to prepare materials such as tutorials for labs and holding remedial classes for students. TA duties should expect to cover 2 x 3 Cr. Hour courses per semester (about 30 x 1.5 hour sessions, or 15 x 3 hour sessions). Assisting the HoD in compiling case studies for publication on Harvard Business School catalog or locally in LUMS catalog or HEC recognized journal such as Asian Journal of Management Cases. (3 Case Studies on E-Commerce/Business Analytics planned) Assist in development of Django based platform for research experiments in area of decision behavior. Assist with development of business analytics research on Punjab IT Board Dengue Patients and Hotspot monitoring database for control of dengue (can learn R libraries for clustering and geographical disease mapping). TA work hours will involve weekends (9am-5pm) and evenings (6pm-10.30pm)

Requirement:

  • Minimum BS with major in Analytics/Data Science or Computer Science from a respectable CS specialist university
  • Expert in writing Python code and experienced in helping others to produce code.
  • Exposure to data science libraries in Python.
  • Ideally has worked on research project during degree and has experience of literature review
  • Good oral and written communication skills.
  • Medium-Advanced experience with Excel (e.g. PivotTables, using lookup functions, relative addressing, etc.)
  • Strong theoretical understanding of programming and designing applications, especially for businesses.
  • Flexible to evening shifts and weekends
  • Minimum 2-4 years of experience with TA'ship during studies.

The deadline to apply for the position is April 25th, 2021.
Interested candidates may apply with position in subject line at hr@ksbl.edu.pk.
