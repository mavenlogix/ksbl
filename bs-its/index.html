KSBL IT&S Program

KSBL BS-IT&S offers 4-years rigorous training to develop technical expertise and mindset of delivering IT as a service so the organization and its employees can respond to changing business environments. Our BS degree in IT and Systems not only prepares students to understand the business problems behind software design, it also equips them with the aptitude to develop innovative ways to manage data. The program will enable young minds to understand information systems holistically and how it incorporates the technology, people and processes involved with information.

Prepare for the Future Job Market

Make the right move to prepare for the job market of the future with rigorous training that would enable you to work in a multitude of areas including designing algorithms, programming, data, robotics, and AI and much more. The curriculum includes foundational learning in the initial years, on to more specialized courses, and in-depth understanding of the business and socio-economic impact of these systems. The training by an experienced local and international faculty culminates in an exciting project in which students demonstrate the use of their technical and business expertise.

IT&S Curriculum

Curriculum for KSBL program establishes the foundation or business and management principles among professionals with industry exposure. Our MBA program incorporates strategic teaching with an emphasis on latest business education, content, interpersonal capabilities and Our faculty goes an extra mile to teach individuals about the modern business ethics & analytic, entrepreneur, design and brand management as well through a comprehensive focal point. Apart from Industrial exposure, mentorship and apprenticeship programs, legacy thrives on investing in people from diverse backgrounds through a holistic approach towards leadership training.
The Program includes 120 credit hours of courses to be completed in 4 years.
