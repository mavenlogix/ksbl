KSBL Organized Networking Night 2020 for senior MBA batches

Tuesday, February 11th, 2020

KSBL held Networking Night 2020, for the senior MBA batches. The annual affair was attended by professionals from renowned corporate organizations who boosted morale of students with their career advices. The companies who attended this event included Nestle Pakistan, Mondelez International, L'Oreal Pakistan, PTCL, Maersk, GSK, Novo Nordisk, Yamaha Motor Pakistan (Pvt.) Ltd., Abbott, Foodpanda, Packages, DH Corporation Limited, Ufone, Multinet Pakistan (Pvt.) Limited, Shan Foods (Pvt.) Ltd., Byco Petroleum Pakistan Ltd., Faysal Bank Limited, ICI Pakistan Limited.

The event started off with students and institute's management welcoming company representatives in first part of the session. All students actively participated during the evening in exchange of unique business ideas and also business cards, to make first-hand business connections.

In second part, all students were divided in groups with different companies pre-assigned to each group. All teams then got a chance to interact with company representatives where they were given fifteen minutes to know about the assigned organization, and convey representatives about their unique competencies and potential.

The evening provided an exclusive platform to students to make new contacts and explore opportunities for rapid career development.
